Dual USB-C Data & Power Delivery

This hub is the only MacBook Pro/Air hub with two USB-C ports
that supports power (PD) and data (40 Gbps/100W + 5 Gbps/60W).

Pure 4K HDR video output + 4K Mini DisplayPort

Experience clear 4K HDMI/MiniDP video on compatible 4K monitors
for brilliant high-definition viewing.
Requires a direct HDMI-to-HDMI or MiniDP-to-MiniDP/DP connection.
